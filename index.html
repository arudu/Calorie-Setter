What is this based off of? This formula is called the Aragon formula a simple formula of calories = TBW * (number of training sessions a week + ITF), TBW = Total Bodyweight and ITF = Itensity Training Factor. The ratios are as follows.

  • Fatloss = BW * 12-14
  • Maintenance = BW * 15-16
  • Weight Gain = BW * 17+
